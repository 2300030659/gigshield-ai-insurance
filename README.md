# 🚀 EarnShield AI - AI-Powered Parametric Insurance for Grocery Delivery Partners

## 📌 Problem Statement

Grocery delivery partners (Zepto, Blinkit, Swiggy Instamart) depend on active working hours to earn income.  
However, external disruptions such as heavy rain, heatwaves, and pollution can suddenly halt their work, leading to immediate income loss.

Currently, there is **no real-time income protection system** for these gig workers.

---

## 💡 Solution

EarnShield AI is an **AI-powered parametric insurance engine** that provides **instant income protection**.

Unlike traditional insurance:

❌ No paperwork  
❌ No manual claims  
❌ No waiting period  

✅ Automatic detection  
✅ Zero-touch claims  
✅ Instant payout  

The system continuously monitors disruptions and automatically compensates workers.

---

## 👤 Persona

- Grocery Delivery Partner (Zepto / Blinkit / Instamart)
- Works in quick commerce (10–15 min delivery)
- Income depends on daily working hours

---

## 🧠 AI Model (Rule-Based Risk Engine)

We implemented a **rule-based AI model** to dynamically calculate risk and weekly premium.

### Inputs:
- 📍 Location (city / industrial / rural)
- 🌦 Environmental risk (simulated)
- ⏱ Working hours

### Risk Score Calculation:
- Area risk → +1 to +3
- Activity (hours) → +1 to +3
- Environmental simulation → +1 to +3

### Output (Weekly Pricing Model):
- Low Risk → ₹20/week
- Medium Risk → ₹35/week
- High Risk → ₹50/week

👉 This ensures **affordable + risk-based pricing**
